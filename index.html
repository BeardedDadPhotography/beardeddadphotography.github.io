About the Photographer

The Man
Behind the Lens

BeardedDad Photography was born out of two of the most pivotal moments in my life — the passing of my father in 2014 and my divorce in 2020 after a 20-year marriage. Both events shook me deeply, and I found myself needing an outlet — not just to heal, but to rediscover who I was.

That's when I picked up my dad's camera.

Photography became more than a hobby — it became a form of therapy, expression, and connection. With every photo I took, I found myself engaging with people, telling their stories, and capturing fleeting moments that often go unnoticed.

One moment that truly solidified my passion was at my dad's funeral. As we prepared a photo collage to celebrate his life, I realized something painful — I wasn't in many of the photos. That moment stuck with me. I made it my mission to ensure that others don't go through life unseen the way I had. So no one is left out of the frame.

Based in Severance, Colorado — inspired daily by the Rocky Mountains, clear Colorado skies, and the stories of the people I meet.
